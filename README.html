PSXm

PSXm statistical methods for Windows 3.1, beta version v2.1-beta from 1995. Developed in MS Visual C++ 1.0 (see Microsoft Corporation, 1993, also e.g. Ettinger, 1994).

Designed for the application of the fundamental and most important mathematical-statistical and probabilistic methods in the context of scientific psychology, in the field of research and diagnostics, see e.g. Bortz and Weber (2005), Bortz and Schuster (2010), respectively.
